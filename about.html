A Husband & Wife Team Who Truly Care

QualityTex isn’t a faceless company with a call centre and a clipboard. It’s us — a husband-and-wife team who pour our heart and soul into every wall, every ceiling, and every room we touch. We started this business because we believe your home deserves more than “good enough.” It deserves craftsmanship that makes you stop in the doorway and smile.

Between us we bring decades of hands-on experience in plastering and decorating, but what really sets us apart is the way we work. We treat every home as if it were our own. We turn up on time, we keep things spotless, and we don’t cut corners — ever. When we say a job is finished, we mean it’s finished to a standard we’d be proud to live with ourselves.

Based in Totton and serving Southampton and the wider Hampshire area, we’ve built our reputation one happy customer at a time. No flashy advertising, no gimmicks — just honest, exceptional work and the kind of personal service that only a family business can offer. From a single feature wall to a full house renovation, we give every project the same care and attention to detail.

We’d love to hear about your project. Give us a call or drop us a message — the kettle’s always on.
